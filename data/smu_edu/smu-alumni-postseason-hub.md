# SMU Bowl Game Fan Guide
The Mustangs are headed to beautiful San Diego! SMU will take the field in the Holiday Bowl on January 2, 2026, and we want you to be part of the excitement. Whether you’re traveling to California or cheering from home, this fan guide has everything you need – game details, fan events, watch parties and more. Secure your tickets, join the fun, and make this bowl game unforgettable! 
[ Game & Ticket Info ](https://smumustangs.com/feature/postseason-central-2025) [ Watch Parties ](https://www.smu.edu/alumni/events/regional-watch-parties)

## SMU Fan Zone
**Date:** Friday, January 2, 2026  
**Time:** 2–4:30 p.m. PT  
**Location:**  
Snapdragon Stadium “East Lawn”  
2101 Stadium Way  
San Diego, CA 92108  
**Cost:** $50 per person (children under 8 are free) 
Bring the Boulevard energy to San Diego. Join alumni, parents, friends and fans at the official **SMU Fan Zone** before the **Holiday Bowl**. Enjoy game-day food, a high-energy SMU atmosphere and Mustang swag as you get ready for kickoff. 
A portion of your registration supports the **Student–Athlete Benefit Fund**. Online registration is preferred by Wednesday, December 31.

## Postseason FAQ
[ Where should I buy tickets? ](https://www.smu.edu/alumni/events/smu-alumni-postseason-hub#faq-a1)
All official ticket information is available on [SMU Postseason Central](https://smumustangs.com/feature/postseason-central-2025). To sit in the SMU sections for the Holiday Bowl, purchase your tickets through the [official SMU ticket site for the Holiday Bowl](https://smumustangs.evenue.net/list/FB-HOL). 
[ Is there an official SMU fan hotel? ](https://www.smu.edu/alumni/events/smu-alumni-postseason-hub#faq-a2)
Yes. The SMU fan hotel is the **Manchester Grand Hyatt** in San Diego. A limited block of rooms is available; book through the [Manchester Grand Hyatt SMU hotel block](https://www.hyatt.com/events/en-US/group-booking/SANRS/G-HB5F). 
[ What are my parking and transportation options for game day? ](https://www.smu.edu/alumni/events/smu-alumni-postseason-hub#faq-a3)
  * **Parking passes:** Purchase in advance at [Ticketmaster](https://www.ticketmaster.com/parking-holiday-bowl-san-diego-california-01-02-2026/event/0A0063339B311D6C). 
  * **Recommended lot:** The **Orange Lot** is recommended for proximity to the SMU Fan Zone on the East Lawn. 
  * **Rideshare:** Drop-off and pick-up are at the **Purple Lot** , a short walk to the SMU Fan Zone. 
  * **Trolley (Green Line):** The trolley stop is about a 5-minute walk to the SMU Fan Zone. Plan your trip via [MTS Snapdragon Stadium information](https://www.sdmts.com/getting-around/snapdragon-stadium). 


[ Will there be alumni events around the game? ](https://www.smu.edu/alumni/events/smu-alumni-postseason-hub#faq-a4)
SMU works with campus partners and local leaders to offer SMU Fan Zone experiences and official watch parties when possible. Availability depends on location and timing, but this page will always show the most current opportunities to gather with fellow Mustangs. 
[ How will I know when registration opens or details change? ](https://www.smu.edu/alumni/events/smu-alumni-postseason-hub#faq-a5)
Registration links for the SMU Fan Zone and any watch parties will be posted on this page and shared by email with alumni and parents. To help ensure you receive invitations and updates, please keep your contact information current with SMU. 
**Questions?** Contact SMU Alumni Relations at 214-768-2586 or smualum@smu.edu.
