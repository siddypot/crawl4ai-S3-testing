### Quick Links
![Exterior of Davidson-Gundy Alumni Center at night. ](https://www.utdallas.edu/files/2022/06/dgac-evening-1280-1024x575.jpg)
The [Office of Development and Alumni Relations](https://development.utdallas.edu/) is dedicated to keeping generations of Comets connected to their alma mater and fostering relationships with friends who support the University. There are more than [150,000 alumni](https://alumni.utdallas.edu/) representing UT Dallas across the globe.
The [Davidson-Gundy Alumni Center](https://davidson-gundy.utdallas.edu/) is a touchpoint and on-campus home for alumni, and a premier location for events.
