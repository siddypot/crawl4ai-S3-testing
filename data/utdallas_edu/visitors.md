## UTD Visitor Center
The UTD Visitor Center is now located at [2830 Rutford Ave., Richardson, Texas 75080](https://map.concept3d.com/?id=1772#!m/1057657?share).
[Sign up today](https://enroll.utdallas.edu/visit/tours/) for a campus tour!
For more information, go to the [Visit UT Dallas page](https://enroll.utdallas.edu/visit/), call [972-883-6046](tel:1-972-883-6046) or email admission@utdallas.edu
## Plan a Visit
The best way to picture yourself at UT Dallas is to visit. [Guided campus tours](https://enroll.utdallas.edu/visit/tours/) are offered at the UTD Visitor Center for undergraduate prospective students. Prospective graduate students should contact their department directly to arrange a visit.
You also can explore the campus at your own pace on a [self-guided tour](https://map.concept3d.com/?id=1772#!ct/42140,88641,60849,82008,88642,88643?s/?t/2711:1?share).
Visitors can get a one-day parking pass from the Parking Office ([PS3 1.200](https://map.concept3d.com/?id=1772#!m/610637)) with a photo ID. This pass may be used on the day it is issued and will allow you to park in green-marked parking spaces.
