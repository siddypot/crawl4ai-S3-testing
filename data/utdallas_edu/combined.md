See for yourself what makes the UT Dallas campus a special place. Take a tour and experience the iconic Margaret McDermott Trellis Plaza, signature reflecting pools lined with magnolia trees, plus sustainable spaces that include campus butterfly gardens and beehives.
## UTD Visitor Center
The UTD Visitor Center is now located at [2830 Rutford Ave., Richardson, Texas 75080](https://map.concept3d.com/?id=1772#!m/1057657?share).
[Sign up today](https://enroll.utdallas.edu/visit/tours/) for a campus tour!
For more information, go to the [Visit UT Dallas page](https://enroll.utdallas.edu/visit/), call [972-883-6046](tel:1-972-883-6046) or email admission@utdallas.edu
## Plan a Visit
The best way to picture yourself at UT Dallas is to visit. [Guided campus tours](https://enroll.utdallas.edu/visit/tours/) are offered at the UTD Visitor Center for undergraduate prospective students. Prospective graduate students should contact their department directly to arrange a visit.
You also can explore the campus at your own pace on a [self-guided tour](https://map.concept3d.com/?id=1772#!ct/42140,88641,60849,82008,88642,88643?s/?t/2711:1?share).
Visitors can get a one-day parking pass from the Parking Office ([PS3 1.200](https://map.concept3d.com/?id=1772#!m/610637)) with a photo ID. This pass may be used on the day it is issued and will allow you to park in green-marked parking spaces.

# Galaxy
The Galaxy portal is the starting point for working in the Orion system, Gemini FMS and Gemini HCM. It also provides links to other services such as email and eLearning.
![](https://sites.utdallas.edu/enroll/files/2022/06/loudspeaker-cone.svg)
**Tips before logging into Galaxy**
  * Clear all browser cache. [Knowledge article for instructions](https://atlas.utdallas.edu/TDClient/30/Portal/KB/ArticleDet?ID=49).
  * Remove any bookmarked links to the Orion, Gemini FMS or Gemini HCM systems. Please access the systems through <https://www.utdallas.edu/galaxy/>. 


**Routine Maintenance:** Orion, Gemini FMS and Gemini HCM may be unavailable due to routine maintenance on Sunday mornings between 2 a.m. and noon. 
****Extended Maintenance:** The Office of Information Technology (OIT) will perform extended maintenance on the Orion and Gemini systems on Friday, December 5th, from 6 PM to Monday, December 8th at 3 PM CST. During this maintenance window, the PeopleSoft systems of Orion, Gemini HCM, and Gemini FMS will be unavailable.  
  
This maintenance does not affect applications outside of PeopleSoft, such as eLearning or Office 365.
![](https://websvcs.utdallas.edu/shared/svg/tnp/mortarboard-books-orange.svg)

Access to application status, academic records, financial aid and tuition/fees for applicants, students, faculty and staff.

### [Gemini](https://dacs-prd.utshare.utsystem.edu/psp/DACSPRD/?cmd=start)

![](https://websvcs.utdallas.edu/shared/svg/tnp/book-pen-orange.svg)

How-to documents for navigating in the new interface for Orion and Gemini systems.

Having trouble with Orion/Gemini login? Find recommendations and troubleshooting information.
